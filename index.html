Route Description:

Created in 2004, the Sierra route traverses the southwestern desert climates of West Texas, New Mexico, and Arizona before heading north up the California, Oregon, and Washington coasts and into British Columbia and the Yukon before finally reaching Alaska. The route stops in large cities, small towns, and national parks along the way.

Total Mileage: 5,258 miles

    Sierra Route Major Stops:

  • Day 1 (6.04): Lampasas, TX
  • Day 9 (6.12): Tucson, AZ
  • Day 10 (6.13): Phoenix, AZ
  • Day 11 (6.14): Phoenix, AZ
  • Day 16 (6.19): Zion National Park, UT
  • Day 19 (6.22): Los Angeles, CA
  • Day 28 (7.01): San Francisco, CA
  • Day 41 (7.14): Mt. Ranier National Park, WA
  • Day 42 (7.15): Seattle, WA
  • Day 44 (7.17): Vancouver, BC
  • Day 51 (7.24): Burns Lake, BC
  • Day 55 (7.28): Meziadin Junction, BC
  • Day 59 (8.01): Johnson's Crossing, YT
  • Day 60 (8.02): Whitehorse, YT
  • Day 69 (8.11): Wasilla, AK
  • Day 70 (8.12): Anchorage, AK
